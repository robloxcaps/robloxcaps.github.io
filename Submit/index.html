Submit Your Pack

Fill out the form below to submit your pack for review. All submissions are moderated for quality and compatibility.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Packs must be a rbxm file or toolbox model, they cannot be any other file.
  • Include working download links.
  • Use appropriate size icons (100x100px recommended).
  • Provide clear descriptions of what's included.
  • Only submit content you have rights to distribute, unless you got permission from the pack's owner.
  • If your pack doesn't get accepted, you can add it to a caprum you created, the caprum is our forum on our personal discord server click the discord link below!.

What happens next?

  1. Review: We'll test your pack within 24-48 hours
  2. Approval: If approved, we'll add it to our packs database
  3. Notification: You'll be notified via email about the decision
  4. Additon: Your pack will be added in a time frame when we are active, if your pack isn't added then we might reconsindered your pack's additonal to this wonderful website!
